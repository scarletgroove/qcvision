🎯 DETECTION CAPABILITIES
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The system detects 5 types of defects on paper rolls:

  1. 🟤 DARK CONTAMINATION
     Detection: Dark threshold + morphological filtering
     Uses: Foreign dark particles, dirt, ink spots

  2. ⚪ BRIGHT PARTICLES  
     Detection: Bright threshold + contour analysis
     Uses: Dust, lint, white spots

  3. 🔲 SURFACE DEFECTS
     Detection: Edge detection (Canny) + aspect ratio
     Uses: Scratches, tears, holes

  4. 〰️ WRINKLES/FOLDS
     Detection: Laplacian variance + ellipse fitting
     Uses: Surface undulations, creases

  5. 🎨 COLOR ANOMALIES
     Detection: HSV color space analysis
     Uses: Shade changes, fading, bleaching

📞 TROUBLESHOOTING
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: Too many false positives
Solution: Increase CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD (0.7 or higher)
          In Streamlit: Move "Confidence Threshold" slider right

Problem: Missing real defects
Solution: Decrease CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD (0.3 or lower)
          In Streamlit: Move "Confidence Threshold" slider left

Problem: Detecting 'pattern' not 'defects'
Solution: Increase MAX_DEFECT_AREA or CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD

Problem: Slow performance
Solution: Check VIDEO RESOLUTION (should be 640x480 or less)

For detailed help, see: IMPROVEMENT_GUIDE.md
